Air Fryer Meatballs

After trying air fryer meatballs, I will never bake them again!

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces lean ground beef
  • 4 ounces ground pork
  • 1/2 cup grate Parmesan cheese
  • 1/3 cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

How to prepare

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Combine beef, pork, Parmesan cheese, bread crumbs, egg, garlic, Italian seasoning, and salt in a large bowl. Mix until evenly combined
  3. From into 16 equal meatballs(a small ice cream scoop is helpful) and place on a baking sheet
  4. Place 1/2 of the meatballs in the basket of the air fryer and cook 8 minutes. Shake the basket and cook 2 minutes more. Transfer to a surving plate and let rest for 5 minutes. Repeat with remaining meatballs
  5. Serve warm and enjoy!
